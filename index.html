what I do

Three ways I make your business simpler.

Most small businesses are losing time to manual work and money to software they've outgrown. I fix both, and build the custom tools that off the shelf products can't.

01

Automate the busywork

Your team is spending hours on repetitive, manual tasks. I find them and automate them, so those hours go back to work that actually matters.

02

Replace pricey subscriptions

Outgrown a SaaS tool, or only using a sliver of it? I build a lean custom replacement you own outright, and the monthly bills stop.

Websites and platforms

A brand new site, a move to a better platform, or fixes to what you already have. I build sites that look sharp and load fast, on whatever tools fit you best.

Custom development

When nothing off the shelf fits, I build it from scratch: web apps, internal tools, and products shaped around exactly how you work.
