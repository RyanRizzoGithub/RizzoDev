services

What I build

Everything is scoped, priced, and explained up front. You get clean, documented work and a developer who tells you the simplest path to what you actually need.

01

Build modern websites

Whether you need a brand new site, a move to a better platform, or fixes and upgrades to what you already have, I design and build sites that look sharp and load fast. I work across the tools you might already be using, so you are never boxed into one.

  • New sites built from the ground up
  • Migrations to a better platform
  • Updates and fixes to your current site
  • Fast, mobile friendly, and easy for you to update
02

Automate the busywork

Your team is burning hours on repetitive, manual work: rekeying data, copying between tools, chasing the same updates, building the same reports. I map those time sinks and automate them, so your people get their time back for the work that actually grows the business.

  • Repetitive data entry & transfer between systems
  • Automated reports, reminders & notifications
  • Document, invoice & email generation
  • Scheduled jobs and back office workflows
03

Replace pricey subscriptions

Paying a growing monthly fee for software you've outgrown, or only use a fraction of? I build a lean custom tool that does exactly what you need and nothing you don't. You own it outright, it fits the way you already work, and the recurring bills stop. And when a subscription really is the smarter buy, I'll tell you that too.

  • Custom replacements for overpriced SaaS
  • Only the features you actually use
  • One build instead of forever per seat fees
  • You own the code, no lock in
04

Develop custom software

When nothing off the shelf fits, I build from scratch: web apps, internal tools, and customer facing products, designed around exactly how your business runs. Clean, documented, and built to last, with the integrations to connect everything you already use.

  • Web and internal applications
  • Customer portals and dashboards
  • Integrations with the tools you already use
  • Clean, documented, maintainable code
