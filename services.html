services

What I build

Everything is scoped, priced, and explained up front. You get clean, documented work and a developer who tells you the simplest path — not the most expensive one.

01

Automate the busywork

Your team is burning hours on repetitive, manual work — re-keying data, copying between tools, chasing the same updates, building the same reports. I map those time-sinks and automate them, so your people get their time back for the work that actually grows the business.

  • Repetitive data entry & transfer between systems
  • Automated reports, reminders & notifications
  • Document, invoice & email generation
  • Scheduled jobs and back-office workflows
02

Replace pricey subscriptions

Paying a growing monthly fee for software you've outgrown — or only use a fraction of? I build a lean custom tool that does exactly what you need and nothing you don't. You own it outright, it fits the way you already work, and the recurring bills stop. And when a subscription really is the smarter buy, I'll tell you that too.

  • Custom replacements for over-priced SaaS
  • Only the features you actually use
  • One build instead of forever per-seat fees
  • You own the code — no lock-in
03

Custom development

When nothing off-the-shelf fits, I build from scratch — web apps, internal tools, customer-facing products — designed around exactly how your business runs. Clean, documented, and built to last, with the integrations to connect everything you already use.

  • Web & internal applications
  • Client / customer portals & dashboards
  • Integrations with the tools you already use
  • Clean, documented, maintainable code
