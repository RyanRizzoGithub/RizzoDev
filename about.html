about

Hi, I'm Ryan.

I'm a computer science graduate who started RizzoDev because I love two things: solving new problems, and helping people. Small businesses are where those overlap: real problems, real impact, and a chance to make someone's day genuinely easier.

I'm not an agency or a faceless contractor. You work directly with me, you get straight answers, and you get software that's built with care down to the detail.

I'm also endlessly curious and always building: data tools, generative art, music experiments, small games. That habit of figuring things out is what I bring to your project. I learn fast, sweat the details, and don't stop until it works.

Why RizzoDev exists

After earning my CS degree, I kept noticing the same thing: small businesses were stuck with tech that was too expensive, too complicated, or just didn't fit. They didn't need more buzzwords, they needed someone who could listen, build the right thing, and explain it clearly.

That's RizzoDev. Expert work without the ego. Software that makes your business simpler, more efficient, and easier to run, with someone you can actually reach when you need them.
